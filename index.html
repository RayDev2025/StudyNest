Design Concept

Built like a nest — warm, safe, and made for growth

StudyNest is inspired by the image of a nest: a place of comfort, safety, and learning. The platform uses a bold gold and black palette with a refined design to create a focused study environment.

  • ✓Designed for senior high school and college students
  • ✓Organized to reduce study overwhelm and confusion
  • ✓Accessible across all devices and screen sizes
  • ✓Promotes a comfortable digital learning experience
